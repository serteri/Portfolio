CODE DESIGNERS SHOULD CODE: BUILD AN APP FROM SCRATCH

A breakdown of a simple app, from UI design to deployment, that shows off why coding is a magic tool for designers. Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, Wacom Tablet, sketchbook… all tools for interfaces and web designers, yes? Take 2 minutes, and try to remember why you want to become a designer and why you enjoy designing stuff. Chances are it’s because you like to create; you’re a creative person. Maybe you started with artistic experiences as a child, then turned that creative energy into problem-solving while continuing to express it visually: You became a designer, a creative problem solver.
